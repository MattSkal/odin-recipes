Baked potatoes

Recipe ingredients

  • 1 baking potato

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step

    3. Scrub the potato and prick it with a fork to prevent steam from building up and causing the potato to explode in your oven.

  3. Step

    4. Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
